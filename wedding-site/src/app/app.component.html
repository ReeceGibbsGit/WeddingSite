Our Story

It was 2020 when we first met at a party while Georgia was meant to be studying for a psychology test. Sparks flew instantly when Reece impressed her with his banter, and plans were made to meet up again the next weekend. Frustratingly, the world then went into lockdown, forcing some rather inventive covert rendezvous. It was in this time of texting (until 3am some mornings) that we discovered a mutual love of deep chats over good coffee, a passion for travel, and surprisingly complementary tastes in music.

Since then our love has grown, with our life adventure finally ending up with us both living in New Zealand, after a year of being 12 000km apart. Going from long-distance movie dates, managing time zones and visas; we now spend our time exploring hiking trails, trying out new recipes, and finding the best spots for cocktail sundowners.

In August, Reece proposed to Georgia who has still not stopped staring at the stunning ring he designed for her. Another design of his is this website you are currently reading: he coded it all from scratch (and Georgia thinks it's brilliant)! We are beyond excited to start this new chapter by celebrating our love and future together with all of our favourite people!

The Venue

We will be celebrating the first day of our marriage at Haycroft Farm, located in The KZN Midlands.

Weather-permitting, the ceremony will be held in the Forest Chapel; a breath-taking clearing under the forest canopy, overlooking the water.

Thereafter, drinks, canapes, and cake will be enjoyed on the deck before heading to the reception for festivities.

Getting to the Venue

From the N3:
Take Exit 107 towards Tweedie
Turn right onto R103
After ±1km, turn right onto P162 (this is easy to miss)
Continue straight for ±4km (travelling over the N3)
Turn left into Haycroft

This is the most direct route that misses most dirt roads and potholes.
If travelling South-bound, avoid going off earlier exits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact you?

Please reach out to us at georgiareecewedding@gmail.com or via Whatsapp at +64225037456.


Can I bring a plus-one?

Due to budget constraints and space limitations at our venue, we ask that you please stick to the guests specifically named in your invitation.


Can I bring my kids?

Whilst we love little ones, we kindly request a child-free wedding.


What will the dress code be?

Cocktail attire. A step above smart-casual. Jackets requested for men (tie optional). For women, we suggest anything from a cocktail to floor-length dress. As there is grass at the venue, we recommend a wider-heeled shoe for comfort.


What if I have specific dietary requirements?

If you have any specific dietary requirements, please reach out to us directly and we will assist with your request.


Can I take photos?

While we kindly request that our ceremony be unplugged. We would love for you to take as many photos as possible during our reception and share them on the Google Drive folder found here.


Will there be a bar?

Of course! While we are delighted to offer a limited selection of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages on-the-house, we ask that you please bring cash/card should you wish to purchase drinks beyond our provided selection.


What time should I arrive?

We ask that you arrive at least 30 minutes in advance - by 14:30 - for the ceremony to start at 15:00. Cocktails beside the dam and reception to follow. Festivities end at 01:00.
