Our Story

It was 2020 when we first met at a party while Georgia was meant to be studying for a psychology test. Sparks flew instantly when Reece impressed her with his banter, and plans were made to meet up again the next weekend. Frustratingly, the world then went into lockdown, forcing some rather inventive covert rendezvous. It was in this time of texting (until 3am some mornings) that we discovered a mutual love of deep chats over good coffee, a passion for travel, and surprisingly complementary tastes in music. Since then our love has grown, with our life adventure finally ending up with us both living in New Zealand, after a year of being 12 000km apart. Going from long-distance movie dates, managing time zones and visas; we now spend our time exploring hiking trails, trying out new recipes, and finding the best spots for cocktail sundowners. In August, Reece proposed to Georgia who has still not stopped staring at the stunning ring he designed for her. Another design of his is this website you are currently reading: he coded it all from scratch (and Georgia thinks it's brilliant)! We are beyond excited to start this new chapter by celebrating our love and future together with all of our favourite people!
